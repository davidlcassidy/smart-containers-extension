# Smart Containers Firefox Extension

**Smart Containers** is a Firefox browser extension designed to enhance your privacy online by automatically opening high-risk tracking websites (like Google and Facebook) to isolated browser containers. By isolating browsing sessions in different containers, it prevents cross-site tracking, ensuring that your activities on one website don't affect or leak into others. The extension includes a dashboard to easily manage your containers and customizing their appearance. This helps you maintain a more secure and private browsing experience.
